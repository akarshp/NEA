About



This is a restaurant recommendation website based in London with the aim of recommending the best possible restaurants in the area based on the preference of the users. Whether their preference is based off their ideal cuisine choice,their preferred location and their preferred rating, this website aims to find the best possible restaurant for the users and to give the users the best experience. It also gives users the opportunity to share their feedback on restaurants that they have visited, giving them an interactive experience
